DnD Multiclass Spell Slots Calculator

Calculate your 5e multiclassed character's spell slot!

Insert the levels of each of your character's classes and find out how many slots your character has.

How it works

This calculator works assuming the rules of the 5e. As the manual states, for two or more magic classes you have to calculate first of all the level of your character keeping in mind that some classes get access to magic at different levels. In fact, the simple calculation is stated as follow

bard + cleric + druid + sorcerer + wizzard + ⌊paladin/2⌋ + ⌊ranger/2⌋ + ⌊fighter/3⌋ + ⌊rogue/3⌋
